## Overview

This project presents an interactive **Market Cart Sales and Profit Dashboard** built using Microsoft Power BI.  
The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profit growth, product category performance, and customer trends across the United States.

The primary goal of this dashboard is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that help businesses understand performance patterns, identify high-performing products, and make data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard includes multiple visualizations such as KPI indicators, geographic analysis, category performance rankings, time-series sales trends, and customer analysis.

# Business Objective

The main objective of this dashboard is to help decision makers monitor and analyze:

• Overall revenue and profit performance  
• Product category performance  
• Regional sales distribution  
• Customer growth trends  
• Monthly sales performance patterns  

By visualizing these metrics in a single dashboard, stakeholders can quickly identify business opportunities and operational challenges.

---

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights two primary performance indicators.

### Total Sales

**$1.9M**

Total revenue generated from all product sales.

Sales increased by **216.68% compared to the previous year**, indicating significant business growth.

---

### Total Profit

**$248K**

Represents the total profit generated from all product categories.

Profit increased by **203.18% year-over-year**, showing strong financial performance.

# Dashboard Components

## Sales by Category

This bar chart ranks product categories based on total sales revenue.

### Top Performing Categories

| Category | Sales |
|--------|------|
| Phones | Highest |
| Chairs | $191K |
| Storage | $169K |
| Binders | $168K |
| Tables | $167K |

### Insight

Phones generated the highest revenue among all product categories, indicating strong consumer demand in the electronics segment.

Lower-performing categories such as labels and fasteners may require marketing strategies or product optimization.

# Sales by State

### Insight

States with darker shading represent higher sales performance, indicating strong regional demand.

# Profit by Year

This chart compares profit performance across different product segments over time.

### Segments Analyzed

• Furniture  
• Office Supplies  
• Technology  

### Insights

- Profit steadily increased from **2021 to 2023**
- The **Technology segment** consistently generated the highest profit
- Slight profit decline is observed in 2024, suggesting potential changes in market demand or operational costs

---

# Monthly Sales Trend

This time-series line chart shows how sales fluctuate throughout the year.

### Insights

• Sales increased significantly toward the end of the year  
• Seasonal demand may influence purchasing behavior  
• October shows a noticeable dip followed by a strong recovery in November and December

This pattern may indicate holiday season demand.
